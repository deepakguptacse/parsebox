===================================
Data Scientist - Risk Analytics
===================================

Company: Meridian Capital Partners
Industry: Financial Services / Investment Banking
Location: New York, NY (On-site)
Employment Type: Full-time
Compensation: $155,000 - $195,000 base + 15-25% annual bonus

Overview:
Meridian Capital Partners is a leading mid-market investment bank seeking a Data Scientist
to join our Risk Analytics group. You will develop quantitative models to assess portfolio
risk, detect anomalies in trading patterns, and build predictive systems for credit risk
evaluation.

Responsibilities:
- Build and deploy machine learning models for credit risk scoring
- Develop real-time anomaly detection systems for trading surveillance
- Create statistical models for portfolio stress testing
- Collaborate with traders and risk managers to translate business needs into analytical solutions
- Present findings to senior leadership and regulatory stakeholders

Requirements:
- Master's degree or PhD in Statistics, Mathematics, Physics, or related quantitative field
- 3+ years of experience in data science, preferably in financial services
- Expert-level Python and SQL skills
- Experience with time-series analysis and forecasting methods
- Proficiency with scikit-learn, XGBoost, and PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Knowledge of financial instruments (derivatives, fixed income, equities)
- Experience with regulatory frameworks (Basel III, Dodd-Frank) is a plus
- Strong statistical foundation (hypothesis testing, Bayesian methods)

Benefits:
- Performance bonus (15-25% of base salary)
- Health and dental insurance
- 20 days PTO + 10 holidays
- Gym membership reimbursement
- Commuter benefits
- Annual offsite retreat
