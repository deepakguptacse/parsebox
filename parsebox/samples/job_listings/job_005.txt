Machine Learning Engineer
MedVision Health

Location: Boston, MA (Hybrid)
Department: AI Research and Engineering
Employment: Full-time
Salary Range: $160,000 - $200,000

Company Background:
MedVision Health is a healthcare AI company developing FDA-cleared diagnostic tools
powered by deep learning. Our products are used in over 300 hospitals across the US
and Europe, helping radiologists detect early-stage cancers with greater accuracy.

Position Summary:
We are seeking an ML Engineer to join our core AI team. You will develop, optimize,
and deploy deep learning models for medical image analysis. This role bridges research
and production -- you will take models from prototype to clinical deployment while
ensuring they meet the rigorous quality standards required for medical devices.

Requirements:
- M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
- 3+ years of hands-on experience training and deploying deep learning models
- Strong PyTorch skills (model development, custom training loops, distributed training)
- Experience with computer vision architectures (CNNs, Vision Transformers, U-Net variants)
- Familiarity with medical imaging formats and standards (DICOM, HL7 FHIR)
- Experience with model optimization (quantization, pruning, ONNX export)
- Understanding of FDA software validation requirements (IEC 62304) is a strong plus
- Publications in top ML/CV venues (NeurIPS, ICML, CVPR, MICCAI) preferred

Description:
You will work on developing next-generation diagnostic models, improving inference
latency for real-time clinical use, building robust data pipelines for multi-site
medical imaging data, and collaborating with clinical partners to validate model
performance in prospective studies.
