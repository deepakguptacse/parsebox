## Sector Themes

### AI/ML Infrastructure (Hot)
The AI wave is real but we're being selective. Avoid "AI wrapper" companies -- look for
proprietary models, unique data moats, or novel architectures. CodeWeave and NightOwl
exemplify what we want: purpose-built models that outperform general-purpose LLMs in
their domain.

### Climate/Energy (Warming)
Breakthrough Energy's involvement in Petajoule validates our thesis that LDES is the
next big clean energy opportunity. GreenLoop is higher risk but the Nature Materials
publication provides scientific credibility. We should aim for 2-3 more climate deals
in Fund III.

### Healthcare AI (Selective)
MediQ is our flagship healthcare bet. The regulatory moat in medical AI is real --
FDA clearance takes 18+ months and clinical validation takes years. This creates a
durable advantage for first movers with published evidence.

### Fintech (Cautious)
PaySync is strong but fintech valuations remain elevated post-2021 correction.
Focus on infrastructure plays (payments rails, compliance) over consumer fintech.

### Enterprise/B2B SaaS (Core)
UrbanPulse shows that "boring" verticals (govtech) can produce excellent businesses.
High retention, long contracts, expansion revenue. More of this please.
